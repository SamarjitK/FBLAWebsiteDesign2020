Sea-Tac International Airport

This international airport has a large variety of fun activities to do in its vicinity, encompassing interests of all ages and sizes. You can go hot air ballooning at Seattle Ballooning, a destination only 2 miles from the airport, or if you want to go further out, the Seattle Light Rail Service within a mile of the airport allows you to do just that. For children, there is the Seattle Chocolate Factory Store and Angle Lake Park where they can enjoy playing in the sun. Those who are more mature can enjoy Boeing's Museum of Flight and go skydiving in IFLY's Indoor Skydiving facility. With all of these destinations within 5 miles of the airport, you're guarenteed to have a good time.
