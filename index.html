Evergreen Destinations

Evergreen can take you to destinations anywhere in the Pacific Northwest.

Sea-Tac International Airport

This international airport has a large variety of fun activities to do in its vicinity, encompassing interests of all ages and sizes. You can go hot air ballooning at Seattle Ballooning, a destination only 2 miles from the airport, or if you want to go visit the Space Needle, the Seattle Light Rail Service within a mile of the airport allows you to do just that. For children, there is the Seattle Chocolate Factory Store and Angle Lake Park where they can enjoy playing in the sun. Those who are more mature can enjoy Boeing's Museum of Flight and go skydiving in IFLY's Indoor Skydiving facility. With all of these destinations within 5 miles of the airport, you're guaranteed to have a good time.

Spokane International Airport

Welcome to the Inland Northwest! If you're have an adventurous spirit, then this airport is for you! Within 10 miles of the airport, you can experience very aspect of nature: You can zipline with Mica Moon Zip Tours, Go strolling in the grandoise Manito Park, Visit the beautiful Spokane Falls, and even Go WhiteWater Rafting on the Spokane River.

Portland International Airport

Are you looking for an escape from the your daily bustling life in the city? Then Portlad International Airport may be the destination for you. This destination offers many quiet, outdoor activities for you to enjoy. With food and drink like the "Great Oregon Wine Tour" you can immerse yourself in the local cuisine. After getting a bike rental from Everybody's Bike Rentals and Tours you can explore the Columbia River Gorge and the "Grotto" a peaceful Catholic sanctuary built with 62 different acres of botanical gardens. With many other small peaceful activities, you'll enjoy Portland as a getaway.

Eugene International Airport

This international airport offers many cultural activities for you to immerse yourself in. Within 10 miles of the airport, you can explore the Oregon Air and Space Museum, the Hult Center of Performing Arts, Museum of National and Cultural History, and even the widely famous Jordan Schnitzer Museum of Art. Coupled with smaller exercusions to local saturday farmer markets or a variety of parks keeps this location well connected.

Rogue Valley International-Medford Airport

If you have a strong connection to nature and thirst for adventure than Medofrd is your ideal trip. Due to its closeness to the Rouge River, this location has many rafting and touring areas surruounding it. You can travel through a variety of lakes (Diamond, AppleGate, and Howard Prairie) and camp or fish to your hearts content. Coupled with many wineries, this location has an excellent local night life to party in.

Boise Airport

Boise offers a multitude of cultural and historical outfits for your family to enjoy. Relive the famous diary in the Idaho Anne Frank Human Rights Memorial and view a history of our armed forces in the Idaho Military History Museum. Add in the availablity to go explore the Rockies, and the availablity of parks such as the Kathryn Albertson park and Julia Davis Park, allows for something for everyone to enjoy.
